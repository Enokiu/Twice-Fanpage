Park Ji-hyo(Korean:박지효; born Park Ji-soo on February 1, 1997), known mononymously asJihyo, is a South Korean singer. She is the leader and vocalist of the South Korean girl groupTwice, formed byJYP Entertainmentin 2015.
She released her debutextended play,Zone, in August 2023, where it debuted at number one on theCircle Album Chartwith half a million copies sold in the first week, breaking the record for the highest initial sales volume for a extended play released by a K-pop female soloist in the chart history so far.[1]The lead single, "Killin' Me Good", charted in five countries.

Life and career[edit]

Early life and pre-debut activities[edit]
Jihyo was born on February 1, 1997, inGuri, Gyeonggi Province, South Korea as Park Ji-soo.[2][3][4]Her younger sister, Lee Ha-eum (born Park Ji-young) is a rookie actress and model.[5]
JYP Entertainmentscouted Jihyo after she placed second in a contest onJunior Naver.[6]She joined the company at the age of eight and trained for ten years,[7][8][9]during which she became the face ofInnisfree's teen line with boy bandBoyfriend,[6]and trained with K-pop acts such asWonder Girls'SunmiandHyerim,Bae Suzy,Jo Kwon, andNichkhun. Jihyo was set to debut in agirl groupwith now-TwicemembersNayeon,JeongyeonandSana, although the project was cancelled.[6][10]Instead, she joined thereality televisionshowSixteen, a competition to select the founding members of Twice.[11][12]She legally changed her name to Jihyo before the competition.[6]As one of nine successful participants, she went on to join the newly formed girl group Twice.[7]Jihyo was voted by her bandmates as leader in an anonymous vote.[6][7][8]

2015–present: Debut with Twice and solo activities[edit]
In October 2015, Twice officially debuted with the release of their first extended play (EP),The Story Begins.[13]Its lead single "Like Ooh-Ahh" became the first K-pop debut song to reach 100 million views onYouTube.[14]On March 6, 2022, Jihyo released her first song for a soundtrack, "Stardust Love Song" fortvN'sTwenty-Five Twenty-One.[15]She released her second soundtrack song titled "I Fly" forSBS'sToday's Webtoonon July 29.[16]On November 14, she released her third soundtrack track "A Strange Day" for theGenie TVandENAseriesSummer Strike.[17]
In January 2023,  Jihyo was announced as the brand model for South Koreancosmeticsbrand Milk Touch.[18]In June 2023, it was announced that Jihyo would release her first EP,Zone, on August 18, 2023, with "Killin' Me Good" serving as the lead single,[19][20]becoming the second member of Twice to make a solo debut.[21]Prior to this announcement, Jihyo performed an unreleased track from the EP, "Nightmare", on Twice'sReady to Be World Tour.[22][23]On August 25, she achieved her first music show win as a soloist
onKBS2'sMusic Bank.[24]

Public image and influence[edit]
On the Korean Business Research Institute's monthly "Individual Girl Group Members Brand Power Ranking" Jihyo placed second and third on the November 2018 and May 2019 search.[25][26]Jihyo has been noted for her vocal and performance skills by the media and fans.[27][28][29][30]
